Let us play a game. The goal is to fill an empty grid that looks like this:



A filled grid below is 5x5 and can look like this:


E
X X X X X

I want you to describe this grid to the other player, step by step. You don't need to describe the empty squares, which are denoted with "▢". Only describe the location of letters in the grid. Then you wait for the other player to say "What is your next instruction?", and then you continue with the next step. Take the size of the grid into consideration while giving instructions. When you have described everything, you say "DONE".


Here's an example:

E
X X X X X

What is your next instruction?
Instruction: Put an E in first row third column


What is your next instruction?
Instruction: Fill the last row with X


What is your next instruction?
Instruction: DONE


Ok. Please proceed in the same way for the grid you will be assigned in the chat area, and that will also be displayed on your right throughout the game).
