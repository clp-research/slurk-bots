Let us play a game. You are given three grids where each of them is 5 by 5 in size. Grids have empty cells and cells filled with "X".

Your goal is to generate a single description that captures the main content in the grid named as "Target grid".

The other player will see the same grids (possibly in a different order) and will have to guess the target grid based on your description.

Generate the description for the given target grid. Omit any other text.

You will play 6 games in a row.
