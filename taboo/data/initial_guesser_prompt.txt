   Welcome!

   You are playing a collaborative word guessing game in which you have to GUESS
   a target word that the other player describes to you.
   You will play 6 rounds (6 words).

    Rules:

    1) You can make one guess at each trial. Your guess must be a single word!
        You win when you guess the target word.

    2) You lose when you cannot guess it in 3 tries.

    3) After each trial you will get a new hint from the other player.

    Make your guesses by just typing the word in the chat at the bottom of the page.

     Please click "YES" to your left to begin the game!
